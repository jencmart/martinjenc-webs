THIS IS FRANCE





In May we passed 10 days on journey in the south France. Just when we realized that the bus drivres were Karel and Karel everyone knew that this would be one of the best trips ever made. And it was so. The weather was fantastic and French were really friendly. And French food was perfect too. Even sightseeing was pretty funny. How I say we enjoyed lot of fun and we were partying every other night ofcourse. Awesomeness is the world which I recalled in my mind when somebody say France. Thanks to everybody who enjoyed this trip so as me... I love you guys !
